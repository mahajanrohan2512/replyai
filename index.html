FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is ReplyAI free to use?

Yes — you get 5 free AI replies with no credit card required. After that, upgrade to Pro for $9.99/month for unlimited replies.

Will the replies sound like AI?

No. ReplyAI is specifically trained to avoid AI clichés like "Great post!" or hollow phrases. Comments are specific to the post content and sound like a real person wrote them.

Which LinkedIn posts does it work on?

All LinkedIn posts — text, image, and video. ReplyAI reads the post content and can even analyse images to generate relevant comments.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your billing portal. No questions asked.

Is my LinkedIn data safe?

ReplyAI only reads the text of the post you're commenting on. We never store your LinkedIn credentials or post history.
